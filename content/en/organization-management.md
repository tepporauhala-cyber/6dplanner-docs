The organization management tool is only available to organization administrators and is used for managing the organization's projects, users, licenses, usage quotas, as well as organization-level libraries and logs.

## Settings

Edit organization's basic information:

- **Logo** - The logo is displayed in the top right corner of all projects. Only available if a special whitelabel license has been purchased.
- **Name**
- **Address**
- **Email addresses** - new users are automatically assigned as organization users if the user's email domain is one of the domains listed here

## Projects

List of all projects created in this organization.

- **Add new project** - Adds a new project under the organization
- **Edit** - Edit the project's basic settings without opening the project
- **Delete** - Deletes the project

## Users

List of all users created in this organization.

- **Role** - The user's role in the organization
