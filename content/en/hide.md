The Hide tool is used to hide parts of existing data sources at project level. For example, you can hide buildings to be demolished from under new ones.

## Hide Objects

- The tool colors objects that you can hide with random colors
- Select in the 3D view the objects you want to hide. Selected objects turn red.
- Select red objects you want back visible, or use the "Show all" button.
- The Save button saves the list of hidden objects

## Hide Area

- The Area button starts the area drawing function
- Draw a polyline around the area you want to hide
- Finally right-click to finish drawing
