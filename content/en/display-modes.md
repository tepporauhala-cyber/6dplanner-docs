---
title: "Display Modes"
description: "Switch between 2D, 3D, GPS, fullscreen and VR modes."
category: "Basics"
order: 2
---

The tool has the following viewing modes:

- **3D** – Default mode that displays the view in 3D perspective mode
- **2D** – Isometric top-down view, also commonly known as "map view". Removes the perspective view.
- **GPS** – Activates the device's GPS positioning and zooms to the device location. GPS mode also has a compass mode that rotates the user's view to the directional device compass points.
- **VR** – Activates dual rendering mode that displays an image rendered for both eyes. Can be used with VR headsets that support such visualization modes.
