The Measurement tool is used to take and mark measurements in the 3D view.

## Measurement Types

- **Point** - Measure coordinates of a single point
- **Distance** - Measure the distance between two points
- **Length** - Measure the total length of a polyline
- **Area** - Measure the area of a polyline region

## Functionality

- Click in the 3D view to set measurement points
- Measurement results are displayed in real time
- Measurements can be saved to the project
