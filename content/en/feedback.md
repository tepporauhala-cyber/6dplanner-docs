The Feedback tool is used to collect feedback from project's internal or external stakeholders, such as the public, local residents, project neighbors, decision-makers and other stakeholders.

## Functionality

- The feedback button in the left toolbar opens the general feedback form with questions
- Feedback "?" icons in the 3D view open location-linked feedback forms
- Each feedback form is different and is defined by the project team
- Answer the feedback questions and press the Submit button
